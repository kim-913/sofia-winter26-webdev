Hi, I'm Kim 👋

Full-time student. Full-time worker. Full-time boba addict. Part-time founder, apparently.

Born in San Jose, Between a Deadline and a Milk Tea Run

Okay real talk — this started as a web dev school project. My assignment was to build a multi-page website for a "realistic client." And I thought: what do I actually care about enough to design 5 pages for?

Boba. Obviously boba.

San Jose already has incredible milk tea — Hey Tea, Moontea, Molly Tea, Wow Tea, and a dozen more spots I've quietly ranked in my head. But between juggling work and school, I kept thinking: what would MY shop look like? The one that's open late, remembers your favorite drink, and doesn't water down the brown sugar.

So Bubble Bliss became that dream — designed at midnight, fueled by the very drinks it's named after. It's a school project that got a little too real. No regrets.

Web Development, Winter 2026. The assignment said "build a website for a realistic client." I built a whole brand. My professor either thinks I'm dedicated or slightly unwell. Possibly both.

Late Night Energy, Always

San Jose Knows Boba

I grew up here. The standard is high. Hey Tea, Moontea, Molly Tea, Wow Tea — all great. Bubble Bliss doesn't try to beat them. It just tries to be exactly what I'd want on a Wednesday at 9pm, with a side of ❤️ saved favorites.
